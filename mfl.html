What is MFL EduClub?

MFL EduClub is an online platform that connects students with a zeal to learn and grow to the mentors who have experience, expertise, and passion to share knowledge in the field of their expertise.

Being an organization that has the vision to bridge the gap between the students and industries which has partnered with your peers like KIIT, ITER, Jadavpur, IIT Bhubaneswar, IIT Madras, IIIT Bhubaneswar, NIT Delhi, DIT, YMCA, DTU, IIT Roorkee.

MFL Educlub is pleased to add Ecell VSSUT as one of its esteemed partners and we look forward to establishing a strong bond between both the organizations.
